The one gesture, two outcomes
Tap
Instant switch to the most-recent other window. No overlay ever appears.
Hold
The overlay appears with a stable list. Release commits; click outside cancels.
Non-negotiable
Stable order. Tiles never reshuffle by recency — Slack is always 4th.
